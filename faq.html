Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BashaKhuji. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us anytime.

General Questions
Is BashaKhuji completely free to use?

Yes! Searching and browsing properties on BashaKhuji is completely free. Creating a tenant account, browsing properties, and contacting owners are all free. Basic property listing for owners is also free.

Does BashaKhuji work all over Bangladesh?

Currently BashaKhuji mainly operates in various areas of Dhaka — including Gulshan, Banani, Dhanmondi, Uttara, Mirpur, Mohammadpur and many more. We plan to expand to Chittagong, Sylhet and other cities in the future.

Does BashaKhuji have any brokers?

No! BashaKhuji is a completely broker-free platform. You can contact the property owner directly without any middlemen, which means no extra cost for you.

For Tenants
Do I need to login to view property details?

Basic property information (photos, location area, price, size) can be viewed without login. However, to contact the owner directly and view the exact address, you need to login with a tenant account. This is for your security.

Can I save a property and view it later?

Yes! Click the "Save Property" button on the property details page and it will be added to your saved list. You can view your saved properties from the Tenant Dashboard.

How do I contact the owner?

Click the "Contact Owner" button on the property details page. After logging in, you will be able to see the owner's phone number and contact information to call or message directly.

How do I report a fake or incorrect property?

For any suspicious listing, email us at: sikderafrida@gmail.com. We will investigate within 24 hours and take action. Your cooperation helps keep our platform safe for everyone.

For Owners
How do I add a property listing?

Login with your owner account and go to the Owner Dashboard. Click "Add New Property" and fill in all property details, photos, and contact information. Our team will verify it after submission.

How long does property verification take?

Properties are usually verified within 24-48 hours. Our team may contact you during verification. Verified properties receive a special badge that increases tenant trust.

How many properties can an owner list?

On the basic plan, an owner can list up to 5 properties. If you need to list more, email us and we will make special arrangements for you.

Payment
Which payment methods are supported?

BashaKhuji supports bKash, Nagad, Rocket, Upay, VISA, Mastercard, DBBL (Dutch Bangla), and SSLCommerz. All transactions are SSL encrypted and completely secure.

Will I get a receipt after payment?

Yes! After every successful payment, a digital receipt will be sent to your registered email. You can also view payment history from your Dashboard.

Security
Is my personal information safe?

Yes, all your personal information is completely safe. We use industry-standard encryption and never share your information with third parties. Read our Privacy Policy to learn more.

What should I do if my account is hacked?

Immediately email us at: sikderafrida@gmail.com. We will help secure your account quickly. You can also reset your password using the "Forgot Password" option.
