What Information Do We Collect?

When you create an account and use our services on BashaKhuji, we collect the following information:

Personal Information: Name, email address, and phone number that you provide during registration.
Property Information: Address, photos, and other details of properties provided by owners.
Usage Information: Anonymous data about which pages you visit and how long you stay — this helps us improve our service.
Device Information: Browser type, IP address, and device information.
We only collect information that is necessary to provide you with our services. We do not ask for any unnecessary data.

How Do We Use Your Information?

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

To create and manage your account.
To facilitate property search and owner-tenant connections.
To improve our services and develop new features.
To send important updates and notifications.
To prevent fraud and illegal activities.

Is Your Information Shared With Others?

We never sell your personal information or use it for advertising. However, limited information may be shared in the following cases:

Owner-Tenant Communication: When you click the "Contact Owner" button, your basic contact information will be shared with the owner.
Legal Obligations: We may be required to provide information under a court order or government directive.
Payment Processor: When making a payment, necessary information is shared with the payment gateway (SSLCommerz).
We never sell or provide your information to third-party advertisers. This is our promise to you.

Information Security

We take the following steps to ensure the security of your information:

SSL Encryption: All data transfers are secured with SSL/TLS encryption.
Password Hashing: Your password is never stored in plain text.
Secure Servers: Our servers follow industry-standard security practices.
Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits to keep your data safe.

Your Rights

As a BashaKhuji user, you have the following rights:

Right to Access: You can view all the information we have stored about you.
Right to Correct: You can correct any incorrect information.
Right to Delete: You can delete your account and all associated data.
Right to Opt Out: You can unsubscribe from any marketing emails at any time.
